Our works

we believe in the importance of protecting our natural world. Our environment is our most valuable resource, and it is up to all of us to preserve it for future generations.

Our environmental initiatives aim to make a positive impact on the health of our planet. From planting trees and cleaning up polluted areas to educating local communities about sustainability, we are committed to making a difference.








Our works and projects

Project Name:-Plant 1 lakh trees in one year. Mission Green Barak has committed to plant 1,00,000 trees every year across its project locations in India. With the monsoon season in full swing, project locations across 11 states. The project teams are steadfast in their commitment to plant the remaining trees before the end of the monsoon, furthering our mission to create a greener, healthier Earth for all. Afforestation is essential for achieving SDG 15 which aims to protect, restore and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, sustainably manage forests, combat desertification, and halt and reverse land degradation and halt biodiversity loss.

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan:- Mission Green Barak is organized swachh bharat abhiyan at karimganj...we had a lot of cooperation from school students in this activity..,karimganj college,govt school,public school,babyland schools,MMMC school ar participating in our swachhata abhiyan program..

ART competition:-Mission Green Barak is organized art competition to celebrate World Environment day 5th june 2023.The foundation is organized the art competition on the theme of BIO-Diversity for encouraging awareness and action to protect our environment.
